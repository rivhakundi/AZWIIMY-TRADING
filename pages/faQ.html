Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What are the health benefits of traditional grains?

Traditional grains like sorghum (mabele) and maize are rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. They are gluten-free, help regulate blood sugar, and support digestive health. Our products are minimally processed to retain these nutritional benefits.

Do you offer wholesale pricing for businesses?

Yes, we offer competitive wholesale pricing for restaurants, retailers, and other businesses. Please use the enquiry form to request our wholesale catalog and pricing information.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver within a 50km radius of our store. For areas beyond this, we can arrange shipping at an additional cost. Contact us for specific delivery options to your location.

How should I store traditional grains?

For optimal freshness, store grains in airtight containers in a cool, dry place. Properly stored, our products can maintain quality for up to 6 months. Refrigeration can extend shelf life further.
